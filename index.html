About the Initiative

Teach people about space through fun projects and create an army of people to fight a space war against the aliens

Mission

Inspire young minds by making space education accessible, hands-on, and community-led.

Vision

To see the next generation develop a genuine passion for space through hands-on learning and exploration.

How we measure impact

Volunteer hours logged, kids reached, final project, feedback from the community
